Editorial Note

The Art of the Silent Guardian

In silk, scent, and stitch, an old wish remains the same: to protect, to steady, and to send good fortune.

Formulation

Specially Blended Fragrance

Mint, patchouli, mugwort, calamus, and sandalwood are balanced into a soft herbal fragrance with a clean, steady finish. The scent feels traditional but easy to live with—fresh in warm weather, gentle indoors, and never overly sharp.

✦

Traditional Protection

In traditional Chinese culture, sachets are often linked with protection, blessing, and warding off negative influences. Mugwort and calamus give the piece a familiar seasonal meaning that feels both cultural and personal.

Daily Calm

The warm woody note of sandalwood adds a quiet sense of calm. Worn on a bag, placed by a bedside, or kept on a desk, the sachet becomes a small companion for slower moments.

Our Story

The Living Heritage

This collection begins with the traditional Eastern sachet: a small object made to carry fragrance, blessing, remembrance, and protection. We translate that idea into a more contemporary form through refined embroidery, botanical filling, and a restrained visual language that still feels warm and human.

Handcraft

Made Slowly, Meant to Stay

Each sachet is finished by hand, from the stitched outline to the final tassel. The process is slow, but that is part of the value: a piece with scent, texture, and symbolic meaning that feels considered enough to keep for yourself or give to someone important.
